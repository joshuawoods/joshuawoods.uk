JOSHUA WOODS
Architectural and Graphic Designer

During a rebranding and name change exercise, I was approached to identify a future brand image and strategy targeted across multiple age ranges, mediums and to unify a number of groups within the church.

A detailed breakdown of brand principles and targets identified a set of requirements to which the new identity was to adhere.

A series of workshops were undertaken and a picture of the brand and future direction began to emerge. An adaptable brand mark was required, from social media logo to long form for banners and reproducable in full colour as well as mono colour.

The final product was the result of a process refining the positioning and visibility of the result in a crowded market.
